    • Maximum Temperature

    This plot shows the general trend of higher temperatures at the equator. The skew of this plot is driven by the sample date; winter in the Northern Hemisphere and summer in the Southern Hemisphere.


    Answers to Your Questions:

  • The poles show no significant heating. However, this evidence is not conclusive, because the study includes no cities at the poles.
  • Yes, many cities within the tropics recorded maximum temperatures below 72 degrees. Chicla, Peru, with an elevation of 12,444 feet, had a high temperature below 44 degrees.
  • Within five degrees of latitude from the equator, the maximum temperatures ranged from 70 to 95 degrees, from pleasant to hot.
